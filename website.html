Saving Oceanic Animals

Many aquatic animals are severely endangered due to the massive amounts of overfishing and capture to feed a hungry population. As we dwelve further into the extinction of these animals, being aware of what you eat is key. This is a website to search for the seafood that you are eatinf right now, and how you can improve your intake of seafood to improve the earth!

Ways that are caught:

Alaskan Salmon are either caught in a responsible manner or are farmed in large aquacultures. Farmers will make large areas of aquaculture in ponds or bodies of water where they will make hundreds of thousands of fish in order to ship out to eat. They are an abundant species to farm and catch, and if are eaten in a super responsible manner.

Is it ECO-friendly for your body?

Salmon is a very nutritious source of omega-3 and is low in contaminants due to the way that it is farmed and caught. It can be a replacement for Bluefin tuna and farmed Atlantic salmon.
